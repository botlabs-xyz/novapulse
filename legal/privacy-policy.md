**Draft date:** July 22, 2026

This is a draft privacy policy for NovaPulse. It is not legal advice and does not become final until Afterparty Bot Labs completes owner and legal review, publishes the responsible legal entity and contact details, and provides an effective date.

## 1. Scope

NovaPulse supports Discord server configuration, moderation, onboarding, verification, logging, and security features selected by server administrators. Questions and requests may be sent through the [Afterparty Bot Labs support server]({{ site.links.support }}). A formal privacy contact will be published with the final policy.

## 2. Information NovaPulse may process

Depending on the features a server administrator enables, NovaPulse may process:

- Discord server, channel, role, user, message, and invite identifiers.
- Usernames or display names needed for configured logs and staff review.
- Server configuration for roles, channels, verification, moderation, and security features.
- Invite attribution and member-join information.
- Information submitted through an introduction form, including only the fields a server chooses to request.
- Verification request status, Discord user ID, and request timestamp.
- Message content, links, and attachment URLs when a server enables NovaTrap or spam detection.
- Moderation, logging, and security-event outcomes.

NovaPulse should not receive passwords, Discord tokens, webhook credentials, identity documents in Discord, private database files, or complete environment files.

## 3. How information is used

Information may be used to provide configured server features, support moderation and onboarding workflows, maintain server-owned logs, prevent abuse, respond to support requests, and maintain reliable service operation.

Server administrators are responsible for choosing appropriate features, permissions, notices, staff access, and lawful purposes for their communities.

## 4. Verification and third parties

Where authorized for a server, <code>/idverify</code> can create a Stripe Identity session and associate the relevant Discord user ID with that session. Stripe processes the verification session outside Discord. NovaPulse does not ask members to upload identity documents directly into Discord.

NovaPulse also relies on Discord for account, server, command, message, role, moderation, and gateway services. Server administrators may configure Discord channels or supported webhook destinations for their own logs.

## 5. Logs and access

Server-owned logs are delivered to destinations selected by a server administrator. They may contain moderation, verification, invite, role, or security information. Server administrators control access to those destinations and should restrict access to the smallest appropriate staff group.

## 6. Retention and deletion

Final retention periods, deletion procedures, export options, and response timeframes have not yet been approved. Until they are published, server administrators should use only the features and logging destinations they need and manage access carefully.

Requests for access, correction, or deletion should first be directed to the relevant server administrator. NovaPulse escalation requests may be sent through the [support server]({{ site.links.support }}); do not send identity documents, passwords, tokens, or unrelated private messages.

## 7. Security and safety

NovaPulse is designed to limit information processing to enabled features, protect sensitive configuration values in normal displays, and keep operational information limited. No service can promise absolute security. Server administrators should grant least-privilege permissions, use staff-only channels for sensitive logs, and maintain a clear moderation and appeal process.

## 8. Changes

This policy may be updated as NovaPulse, its providers, or legal requirements change. Material changes will include an updated effective date and an appropriate notice method when the policy is finalized.

## 9. Items requiring final review

- Responsible legal entity, address, and privacy contact.
- Applicable legal bases and jurisdiction-specific rights.
- Retention periods and deletion or export service levels.
- Stripe Identity access, provider settings, and processor terms.
- Hosting and international-transfer disclosures.
- Minors, age-policy, consent, and verification language.
- Security-incident notification procedure.
- Final policy effective date and acceptance process.
